Mike Bibby - 2002 Sacramento Kings

Mike Bibby was one of the standout players for the 2002 Sacramento Kings, serving as the team’s point guard. He was a key contributor not only as a scorer but also as a facilitator and leader on the floor. Bibby’s clutch performances, particularly in the playoffs, helped the Kings reach the Western Conference Finals that year.

During the 2002 season, Mike Bibby was not only the Kings' primary scorer but also a skilled playmaker. His ability to score from both outside and inside, along with his excellent decision-making, made him a dynamic and versatile player.
