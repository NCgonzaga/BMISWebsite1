2002 Sacramento Kings

Welcome to the page dedicated to the legendary 2002 Sacramento Kings team!

The 2002 Sacramento Kings finished the regular season with a remarkable 61-21 record, earning the top seed in the Western Conference. This team was known for its fast-paced, ball movement-oriented style of play, which became a model for modern basketball. They were led by a star-studded lineup, including:

Mike Bibby (Point Guard)

Doug Christie (Shooting Guard)

Peja Stojaković (Small Forward)

Chris Webber (Power Forward)

Vlade Divac (Center)

While the Kings were considered one of the best teams of the early 2000s, they were unfortunately eliminated in the Western Conference Finals by the Los Angeles Lakers in a heartbreaking seven-game series.
